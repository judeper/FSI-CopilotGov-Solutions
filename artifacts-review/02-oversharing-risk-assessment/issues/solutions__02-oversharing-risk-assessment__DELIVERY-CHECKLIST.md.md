### F1 — SharePoint Advanced Management licensing path is incomplete

- **Severity:** Major

- **Current:** "Validate SharePoint Advanced Management licensing for the tenant or document the approved alternative control path."
- **Issue:** The checklist implies standalone SharePoint Advanced Management licensing is the only expected path. ### F2 — DSPM for AI is now the classic experience

- **Current:** "Validate Microsoft 365 E5 Compliance or equivalent capability for DSPM for AI review and related investigations."

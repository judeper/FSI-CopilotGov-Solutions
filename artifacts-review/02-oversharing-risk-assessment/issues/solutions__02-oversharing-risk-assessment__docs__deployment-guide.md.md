### F3 — Restricted SharePoint Search enablement lacks current limitations

- **Severity:** Major
- **Location:** lines 49, 127
- **Current:** "Restricted SharePoint Search is marked for enablement when configured."
- **Issue:** The guide describes enabling and reverting Restricted SharePoint Search without telling operators that it is a temporary, non-security-boundary feature with a 100-site allowed-list limit and exceptions for recently accessed or directly shared content.

## Findings

### F1 — SharePoint Advanced Management licensing prerequisite is incomplete

- **Severity:** Major
- **Location:** line 5
- **Current:** "SharePoint Advanced Management licensing is required for the intended production posture."
- **Issue:** The prerequisite omits that Microsoft 365 Copilot licensing can grant access to SharePoint Advanced Management features needed for Copilot deployment. Learn lists a base license requirement plus either Microsoft 365 Copilot license or standalone Microsoft SharePoint Advanced Management.
- **Suggested change:** Document the base license requirement and both entitlement paths: Microsoft 365 Copilot license assignment or standalone Microsoft SharePoint Advanced Management license.
- **Source:** https://learn.microsoft.com/sharepoint/advanced-management (last updated 2026-04-23)
- **Quote:** "If your organization has a Copilot license... SharePoint administrators automatically gain access."

### F2 — DSPM prerequisite should not be reduced to E5 Compliance

- **Severity:** Major
- **Location:** line 6
- **Current:** "Microsoft 365 E5 Compliance, or an equivalent licensing combination that enables DSPM for AI investigations, should be available for the tenant."

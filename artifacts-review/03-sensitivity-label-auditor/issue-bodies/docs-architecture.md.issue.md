- **Severity:** Critical

- **Current:** "Graph drive items" with "contentLabel data" and "SharePoint and OneDrive drive items plus `contentLabel` metadata"
- **Issue:** Microsoft Graph documents sensitivity label extraction for SharePoint/OneDrive files through `driveItem: extractSensitivityLabels` and `sensitivityLabelAssignment`, not a `contentLabel` driveItem field.
